About Me

Front-End Designer & Reporter

Pratik Adhikari is a Kathmandu-born Front end designer specializing in crafting engaging and user-centric digital experiences. With a strong passion for bringing ideas to life on the screen, Pratik focuses on front-end design, translating complex visuals into clean, maintainable, and interactive code.

Beyond his expertise in designing Front end design, he possesses a solid understanding of back-end processes, allowing him to create comprehensive solutions for data storage and being a company for reporting. Pratik is committed to continuous learning, staying current with new media, technologies, and best practices to deliver data-oriented and highly functional front end design and reporting solutions.

Driven by a goal to create impactful and accessible designs, Pratik enjoys collaborating on challenging projects to bring creative visions to fruition.
